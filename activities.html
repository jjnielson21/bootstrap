Activities

Things to do while in the Bookcliffs

Hiking

There is so much area to visit and explore in the bookcliffs. There is one area that is great for hiking it is called the roadless area of the bookcliffs. With miles and miles of area with no roads around.

Photography

There is everything from wildlife, landscapes, history, and so many other things to take photos of in the bookcliffs.

ATV Riding

With hundreds of miles of roads to explore you can ride on a different route all day and never see the same terrain.

Hunting

There are many opportunities to hunt in the Bookcliffs. From Elk, Deer, Buffalo, Turkey, and many predators.

Shed Hunting

With the ammount of wildlife in the Bookcliffs there are plenty who shed there antlers each year.

Wildlife Viewing

Lots of Animals to see in the Bookcliffs. In the years I have been visiting there I have seen Elk, Deer, Buffalo, Bear, Mountain Lion, Bobcat, Bighorn Sheep, Turkey, and many others.
